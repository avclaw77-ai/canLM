Why Ottavo

Built different. Built Canadian.

Most AI assistants were built with American users in mind. Ottavo was built to get Canada right.

shield

Your Data Stays in Canada

Every conversation lives on Canadian infrastructure — servers in Canada, under Canadian law. Your personal information is never routed through foreign data centres. Genuine Canadian data sovereignty.

translate

It Actually Speaks Canadian

Ottavo responds in Québécois French, not European French. It understands «courriel», «magasiner», and «souper». It doesn't correct code-switching between English and French — because in Montréal, Ottawa-Gatineau, and New Brunswick, that's perfectly normal.

account_balance

It Knows Canada

Ottavo understands Canadian law, institutions, and life. It knows the CRA, TFSAs, CEGEP, provincial drinking ages, and metric by default. When you ask a question, you get a Canadian answer.

favorite

Built with Canadian Values

Privacy, transparency, and respect for Indigenous communities are baked into how Ottavo was designed. Built to comply with Canada's privacy laws, including Quebec's Law 25 — the most rigorous privacy framework in the country.
