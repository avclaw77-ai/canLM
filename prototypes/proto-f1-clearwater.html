Prototype F1 — Clearwater

Concept: Takes the blue tones from Ocean (without the dark hero) and the DM Serif Display typography from Maple. Everything is light, airy, and sophisticated. The hero features a refined floating-orb animation in soft blues — subtle, mesmerizing, never distracting. Inspired by Anthropic's restraint: lots of white space, centered hero, editorial typography.

Mood: Clear, trustworthy, intelligent. Like looking through still water. The blue palette feels institutional yet warm. DM Serif Display headings give editorial authority while DM Sans body text stays modern and approachable.

DM Serif Display + DM Sans (from Maple) Off-white / Soft blue / Navy text / Blue accents Floating blue orbs — refined organic shapes

Why this works: Centered hero with large serif type feels like an established institution, not a startup. The floating animation adds life without competing for attention. Blue is universally trusted. The grid-gap focus cards (no borders, just white cells separated by hairlines) feel clean and architectural.
