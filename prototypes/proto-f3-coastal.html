Prototype F3 — Coastal

Concept: A warm-cool hybrid — the warmth and approachability of Maple (cream backgrounds, gold accents) combined with Ocean's sophisticated blue palette. Two-column hero with an animated SVG composition. This direction says "established institution" through warm tones while staying modern with steel-blue accents. The gold-line eyebrow detail adds quiet prestige.

Mood: Warm, trustworthy, quietly prestigious. Like a well-designed university or cultural institution site. The blue-gold combination evokes wisdom without stuffiness. Cream backgrounds feel inviting, not clinical.

DM Serif Display + DM Sans (from Maple) Warm cream / Steel blue / Amber gold accents Pulsing concentric rings + orbital nodes

Why this works: The warm-cool split mirrors canLM's dual nature — warm and human-centered, but intellectually rigorous. Gold accents on numbered items and eyebrows create a visual thread throughout the page. Cards have a subtle top-gradient reveal on hover. The two-column hero gives room for both messaging and a visual element.
