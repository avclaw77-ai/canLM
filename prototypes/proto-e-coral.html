Prototype E — Vibrant Coral

Concept: A warm, energetic palette centred on coral, teal, and purple — three colours that create visual excitement without clashing. The hero features an animated geometric mesh that morphs and breathes, with colorful nodes connecting. This is the most "modern tech startup" direction — it says "we're innovative and approachable."

Mood: Energetic, modern, inviting. This is the opposite of dry academic. The center-aligned hero with badge/pill elements borrows from the best SaaS landing pages. The bento-grid research layout makes articles feel like something you'd want to click, not homework.

Why this works for non-academics: This is the friendliest option. Coral is warm and inviting. Teal adds trust. The center-aligned hero feels like a welcome mat, not a wall of text.
