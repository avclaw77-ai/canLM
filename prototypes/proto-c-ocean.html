Prototype C — Ocean Current

Concept: A deep-blue-to-light transition. The hero is a dark oceanic scene with animated particle currents and wave forms — representing data flowing through networks. The page transitions from deep ocean to airy sky-blue, symbolizing AI emerging from complexity into clarity.

Mood: Deep, trustworthy, professional yet dynamic. Blue is the most universally trusted colour. The particle animation feels alive without being distracting — it makes you want to watch it.

Playfair Display (editorial serif) + Outfit (modern geometric) Deep navy → Sky blue → White / Teal accents Flowing particle streams + wave transitions

Why this works for non-academics: The ocean metaphor is intuitive — data flows like water. The dark-to-light page transition creates a sense of journey from "the deep" to "understanding." Editorial list-style research section makes articles feel like newspaper content, not academic papers.
