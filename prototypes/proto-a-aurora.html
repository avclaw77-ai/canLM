Prototype A — Aurora Borealis

Concept: A deep, atmospheric dark palette inspired by the Canadian northern sky. The hero features an animated aurora borealis effect — flowing curtains of light that evoke wonder and discovery. This positions AI research as something beautiful and awe-inspiring, not intimidating.

Mood: Majestic, contemplative, uniquely Canadian. The aurora is an iconic Canadian natural phenomenon that instantly signals "this is a Canadian organization" without clichés like maple leaves.

Fraunces (serif display) + Inter (body) Deep navy / Electric blue / Violet / Mint Animated aurora gradient curtains

Why this works for non-academics: The northern lights are universally beloved. People who may feel intimidated by AI research will feel drawn in by the visual beauty. The serif headings add editorial gravitas without stuffiness.
