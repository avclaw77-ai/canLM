Prototype D — Forest & Gold

Concept: Rich forest green paired with warm gold. Inspired by the Canadian boreal forest — the world's largest intact forest ecosystem. The hero features an animated growing tree of knowledge with golden nodes representing research areas branching out. The dark forest background fades to warm natural tones.

Mood: Grounded, prestigious, organic. Forest green symbolizes growth and Canadian nature. Gold adds a layer of prestige and warmth — like sunlight through the canopy. This combination feels institutional (like a university crest) but not stuffy.

Instrument Serif (elegant) + General Sans (clean modern) Deep forest green / Gold / Warm stone / Natural cream Growing tree visualization with golden nodes

Why this works for non-academics: Nature imagery is calming and familiar. The tree metaphor for knowledge growth is intuitive. Gold creates a sense of value — "this research matters." The two-column focus cards feel organized without being clinical.
