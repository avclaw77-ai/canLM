Prototype B — Maple Warmth

Concept: A light, warm palette inspired by Canadian autumn — rich reds, amber golds, and warm cream backgrounds. This direction embraces boldness and confidence while remaining approachable. The hero features animated floating maple-inspired organic shapes.

Mood: Warm, inviting, distinctly Canadian. The colour palette says "we are proud of who we are." Red connects to the Canadian flag without being literal. Gold adds prestige.

DM Serif Display + DM Sans Warm cream / Canadian red / Amber gold / Rust Floating organic shapes, leaf-inspired geometry

Why this works for non-academics: Warm colours are psychologically inviting. The serif headings feel authoritative (like a trusted newspaper) while DM Sans body text is friendly and readable. Nobody feels intimidated by autumn colours.
