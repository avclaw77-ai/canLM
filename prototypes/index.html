canLM — Design Prototypes

Refined design directions based on board feedback

March 29, 2026 — CTO / Design Team

Round 2 — Refined Prototypes (F1–F3)

Based on board feedback on the first 5 prototypes:

  • Aurora: Rejected — no full black
  • Coral: Rejected — too SLOP
  • Ocean: Blue tones liked, but NOT the dark hero
  • Maple: Animation concept and typography (DM Serif Display) liked — needs refinement

New direction: Combine Ocean's blue tones (light, no dark hero) with Maple's typography and animation concept. Aim for Anthropic-level sophistication — sleek, simple, lots of breathing room.

Original Brief

Make the site more vibrant with better fonts, colour accents, and hero animations that make research fun and approachable.
